What your Pro page can include for free

Everything below works with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. User data is saved inside the browser, so you do not need a paid backend yet.

✍️

Advanced Writing Stats

Word count, character count, sentence count, paragraph count, estimated reading time, speaking time, average word length, and unique vocabulary tracking.

🗂️

Flashcards From Text

Turn pasted writing into instant study cards by pulling out longer words and creating simple front/back prompts automatically.

🔤

Spelling + Vocabulary Practice

Practice difficult words from your own text using spelling challenges and multiple-choice meaning games.

💾

Saved Drafts

Save recent writing sessions locally so the browser remembers them even after refresh, without needing login yet.

🔥

Progress Streaks

Track how many days the user has come back to write or study. This increases habit value without any server cost.

⬇️

Export Tools

Let users download their writing and flashcard decks as text files or JSON so they feel the value of the Pro product.
